Welcome to our 3rd Blues and Roots Festival

Our 2015 Festival will be focusing on Blues music, still packed full of lovely people, delicious food, and entertainment for the kids.
Last year we found ourselves totally exhausted by the hurricane and the music continuing into the small hours. So this year, music will start at 12 noon each day, to midnight on Friday and Saturday, and to 11pm on Sunday. And we'll ask nicely for a bit less wind!
We're also going to have just one main performance space – and plenty of room outdoors for you to chill in what's been described as the most beautiful festival venue ever!

The Franck Ash Band



Julian Piper and the Junkyard angels
Junkyard Angels currently are a three piece with a dynamite rhythm section of Andy Mills on drums and Kevin Dance on bass guitar.
Dedicated to playing Blues and NOT BLUES ROCK - expect to hear the gut bucket raunch of John Lee Hooker’s ‘One Scotch, One Bourbon, One Beer’, and the New Orleans sleaze of ‘Junco Partner’, mixed in with B.B. King’s ‘How Blue Can You Get ?’ and Chuck Berry’s ‘Oh Carol’.
